Variable: list-colors-sort

Color sort order for `list-colors-display'. `nil' means default implementation-dependent order (defined in `x-colors'). `name' sorts by color name. `rgb' sorts by red, green, blue components. `(rgb-dist . COLOR)' sorts by the RGB distance to the specified color. `hsv' sorts by hue, saturation, value. `(hsv-dist . COLOR)' sorts by the HSV distance to the specified color and excludes grayscale colors. `luminance' sorts by relative luminance in the CIE XYZ color space.
