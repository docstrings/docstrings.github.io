Function: font-lock-fontify-block

Fontify some lines the way `font-lock-fontify-buffer' would. The lines could be a function or paragraph, or a specified number of lines. If ARG is given, fontify that many lines before and after point, or 16 lines if no ARG is given and `font-lock-mark-block-function' is nil. If `font-lock-mark-block-function' non-nil and no ARG is given, it is used to delimit the region to fontify.
