Function: minibuffer-inactive-mode

Major-mode. Uses keymap `minibuffer-inactive-mode-map', abbrev table `nil' and syntax-table `minibuffer-inactive-mode-syntax-table'.

This mode runs the hook `minibuffer-inactive-mode-hook', as the final step during initialization.
