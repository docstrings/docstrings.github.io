Function: japanese-zenkaku-region

Convert hankaku' chars in the region to Japanese `zenkaku' chars. `Zenkaku' chars belong to `japanese-jisx0208' `Hankaku' chars belong to `ascii' or `japanese-jisx0201-kana'. Optional argument KATAKANA-ONLY non-nil means to convert only KATAKANA char.

(fn FROM TO &optional KATAKANA-ONLY)
