Function: apropos-mode

Major mode for following hyperlinks in output of apropos commands.

key binding --- -------

TAB forward-button RET apropos-follow ESC Prefix Command SPC scroll-up-command - negative-argument 0 .. 9 digit-argument < beginning-of-buffer > end-of-buffer ? describe-mode g revert-buffer h describe-mode q quit-window DEL scroll-down-command backward-button Prefix Command

In addition to any hooks its parent mode `special-mode' might have run, this mode runs the hook `apropos-mode-hook', as the final step during initialization.
