Variable: zoneinfo-style-world-list

Alist of zoneinfo-style time zones and places for `display-time-world'. Each element has the form (TIMEZONE LABEL). TIMEZONE should be a string of the form AREA/LOCATION, where AREA is the name of a region -- a continent or ocean, and LOCATION is the name of a specific location, e.g., a city, within that region. LABEL is a string to display as the label of that TIMEZONE's time.
