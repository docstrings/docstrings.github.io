Function: table-forward-cell

Move point forward to the beginning of the next cell. With argument ARG, do it ARG times; a negative argument ARG = -N means move backward N cells. Do not specify NO-RECOGNIZE and UNRECOGNIZE. They are for internal use only.

Sample Cell Traveling Order (In Irregular Table Cases)
