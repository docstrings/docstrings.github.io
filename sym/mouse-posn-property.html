Function: mouse-posn-property

Look for a property at click position. POS may be either a buffer position or a click position like those returned from `event-start'. If the click position is on a string, the text property PROPERTY is examined. If this is nil or the click is not on a string, then the corresponding buffer position is searched for PROPERTY. If PROPERTY is encountered in one of those places, its value is returned.

(fn POS PROPERTY)
