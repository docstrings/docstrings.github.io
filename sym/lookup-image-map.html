Function: lookup-image-map

Lookup in image map MAP coordinates X and Y. An image map is an alist where each element has the format (AREA ID PLIST). An AREA is specified as either a rectangle, a circle, or a polygon: A rectangle is a cons (rect . ((x0 . y0) . (x1 . y1))) specifying the pixel coordinates of the upper left and bottom right corners. A circle is a cons (circle . ((x0 . y0) . r)) specifying the center and the radius of the circle; r may be a float or integer. A polygon is a cons (poly . [x0 y0 x1 y1 ...]) where each pair in the vector describes one corner in the polygon. Returns the alist element for the first matching AREA in MAP.

(fn MAP X Y)
