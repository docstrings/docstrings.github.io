Function: japanese-hankaku-region

Convert Japanese `zenkaku' chars in the region to `hankaku' chars. `Zenkaku' chars belong to `japanese-jisx0208' `Hankaku' chars belong to `ascii' or `japanese-jisx0201-kana'. Optional argument ASCII-ONLY non-nil means to convert only to ASCII char.

(fn FROM TO &optional ASCII-ONLY)
