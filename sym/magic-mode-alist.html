Variable: magic-mode-alist

Alist of buffer beginnings vs. corresponding major mode functions. Each element looks like (REGEXP . FUNCTION) or (MATCH-FUNCTION . FUNCTION). After visiting a file, if REGEXP matches the text at the beginning of the buffer, or calling MATCH-FUNCTION returns non-nil, `normal-mode' will call FUNCTION rather than allowing `auto-mode-alist' to decide the buffer's major mode.

If FUNCTION is nil, then it is not called. (That is a way of saying "allow `auto-mode-alist' to decide for these files.")
