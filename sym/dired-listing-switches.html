Variable: dired-listing-switches

Switches passed to `ls' for Dired. MUST contain the `l' option. May contain all other options that don't contradict `-l'; may contain even `F', `b', `i' and `s'. See also the variable `dired-ls-F-marks-symlinks' concerning the `F' switch. On systems such as MS-DOS and MS-Windows, which use `ls' emulation in Lisp, some of the `ls' switches are not supported; see the doc string of `insert-directory' in `ls-lisp.el' for more details.
