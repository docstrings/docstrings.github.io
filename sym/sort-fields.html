Function: sort-fields

Sort lines in region lexicographically by the ARGth field of each line. Fields are separated by whitespace and numbered from 1 up. With a negative arg, sorts by the ARGth field counted from the right. Called from a program, there are three arguments: FIELD, BEG and END. BEG and END specify region to sort. The variable `sort-fold-case' determines whether alphabetic case affects the sort order.

(fn FIELD BEG END)
