Function: scroll-bar-scale

Given a pair (NUM . DENOM) and WHOLE, return (/ (* NUM WHOLE) DENOM). This is handy for scaling a position on a scroll bar into real units, like buffer positions. If SCROLL-BAR-POS is the (PORTION . WHOLE) pair from a scroll bar event, then (scroll-bar-scale SCROLL-BAR-POS (buffer-size)) is the position in the current buffer corresponding to that scroll bar position.
