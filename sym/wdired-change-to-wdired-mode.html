Function: wdired-change-to-wdired-mode

Put a Dired buffer in Writable Dired (WDired) mode.

Uses keymap `wdired-mode-map', which is not currently defined.

In WDired mode, you can edit the names of the files in the buffer, the target of the links, and the permission bits of the files. After typing M-x wdired-finish-edit, Emacs modifies the files and directories to reflect your edits.

See `wdired-mode'.
