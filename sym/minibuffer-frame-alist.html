Variable: minibuffer-frame-alist

Alist of parameters for the initial minibuffer frame. This is the minibuffer frame created if `initial-frame-alist' calls for a frame without a minibuffer. The parameters specified here supersede those given in `default-frame-alist', for the initial minibuffer frame.

You can set this in your init file; for example,

(setq minibuffer-frame-alist '((top . 1) (left . 1) (width . 80) (height . 2)))

It is not necessary to include (minibuffer . only); that is appended when the minibuffer frame is created.
