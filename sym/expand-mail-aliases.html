Function: expand-mail-aliases

Expand all mail aliases in suitable header fields found between BEG and END. If interactive, expand in header fields. Suitable header fields are `To', `From', `CC' and `BCC', `Reply-to', and their `Resent-' variants.

Optional second arg EXCLUDE may be a regular expression defining text to be removed from alias expansions.

(fn BEG END &optional EXCLUDE)
