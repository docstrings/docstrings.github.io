Function: format-subtract-regions

Remove from the regions in MINUEND the regions in SUBTRAHEND. A region is a dotted pair (FROM . TO). Both parameters are lists of regions. Each list must contain nonoverlapping, noncontiguous regions, in descending order. The result is also nonoverlapping, noncontiguous, and in descending order. The first element of MINUEND can have a cdr of nil, indicating that the end of that region is not yet known.
