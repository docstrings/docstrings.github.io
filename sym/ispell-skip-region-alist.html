Variable: ispell-skip-region-alist

Alist expressing beginning and end of regions not to spell check. The alist key must be a regular expression. Valid forms include: (KEY) - just skip the key. (KEY . REGEXP) - skip to the end of REGEXP. REGEXP may be string or symbol. (KEY REGEXP) - skip to end of REGEXP. REGEXP must be a string. (KEY FUNCTION ARGS) - FUNCTION called with ARGS returns end of region.
