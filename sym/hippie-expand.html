Function: hippie-expand

Try to expand text before point, using multiple methods. The expansion functions in `hippie-expand-try-functions-list' are tried in order, until a possible expansion is found. Repeated application of `hippie-expand' inserts successively possible expansions. With a positive numeric argument, jumps directly to the ARG next function in this list. With a negative argument or just C-u, undoes the expansion.
