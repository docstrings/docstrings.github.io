Function: scroll-up-command

Scroll text of selected window upward ARG lines; or near full screen if no ARG. If `scroll-error-top-bottom' is non-nil and `scroll-up' cannot scroll window further, move cursor to the bottom line. When point is already on that position, then signal an error. A near full screen is `next-screen-context-lines' less than a full screen. Negative ARG means scroll downward. If ARG is the atom `-', scroll downward by nearly full screen.
