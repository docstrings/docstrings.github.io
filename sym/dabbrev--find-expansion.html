Function: dabbrev--find-expansion

Find one occurrence of ABBREV, and return the expansion. DIRECTION > 0 means look that many times backwards. DIRECTION < 0 means look that many times forward. DIRECTION = 0 means try both backward and forward. IGNORE-CASE non-nil means ignore case when searching. This sets `dabbrev--last-direction' to 1 or -1 according to the direction in which the occurrence was actually found. It sets `dabbrev--last-expansion-location' to the location of the start of the occurrence.

(fn ABBREV DIRECTION IGNORE-CASE)
