Function: gnus-delay-article

Delay this article by some time. DELAY is a string, giving the length of the time. Possible values are:

* for in minutes (`m'), hours (`h'), days (`d'), weeks (`w'), months (`M'), or years (`Y');

* YYYY-MM-DD for a specific date. The time of day is given by the variable `gnus-delay-default-hour', minute and second are zero.

* hh:mm for a specific time. Use 24h format. If it is later than this time, then the deadline is tomorrow, else today.

(fn DELAY)
