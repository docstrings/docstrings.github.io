Variable: comment-inline-offset

Inline comments have to be preceded by at least this many spaces. This is useful when style-conventions require a certain minimal offset. Python's PEP8 for example recommends two spaces, so you could do:

(add-hook 'python-mode-hook (lambda () (set (make-local-variable 'comment-inline-offset) 2)))

See `comment-padding' for whole-line comments.
