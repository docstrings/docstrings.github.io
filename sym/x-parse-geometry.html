Function: x-parse-geometry

Parse a display geometry string STRING. Returns an alist of the form ((top . TOP), (left . LEFT) ... ). The properties returned may include `top', `left', `height', and `width'. For X, the value of `left' or `top' may be an integer, or a list (+ N) meaning N pixels relative to top/left corner, or a list (- N) meaning -N pixels relative to bottom/right corner. On Nextstep, this just calls `ns-parse-geometry'.

(fn STRING)
