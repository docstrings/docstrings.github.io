Function: wordstar-mode

Major mode with WordStar-like key bindings.

BUGS: - Help menus with WordStar commands (C-j just calls help-for-help) are not implemented - Options for search and replace - Show markers (C-k h) is somewhat strange - Search and replace (C-q a) is only available in forward direction

No key bindings beginning with ESC are installed, they will work Emacs-like.

The key bindings are:
