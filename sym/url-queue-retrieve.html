Function: url-queue-retrieve

Retrieve URL asynchronously and call CALLBACK with CBARGS when finished. This is like `url-retrieve' (which see for details of the arguments), but with limits on the degree of parallelism. The variable `url-queue-parallel-processes' sets the number of concurrent processes. The variable `url-queue-timeout' sets a timeout.

(fn URL CALLBACK &optional CBARGS SILENT INHIBIT-COOKIES)
